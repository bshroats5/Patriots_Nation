The Patriots Women’s Soccer team is playing for a National Title at 1 PM tomorrow against a familiar foe, Keiser University.

### Meet Our Challenger

Keiser University, located in West Palm Beach, FL, enters the title match with a 19-3-1 record and is ranked #3 in the latest coaches poll.  
They’ve won two national titles (2019, 2020) and were runners-up last year.

### Meet the Patriots

The University of the Cumberlands (Williamsburg, KY) comes in at 17-4-3 and is ranked #9 in the coaches poll.  
They claimed their first national championship in 2023.

Nine players from that 2023 title team return this season:

1. Nicole Araujo  
2. Abigail Little  
3. Laura Bosca  
4. Carola Fontan  
5. Samantha Sokolove  
6. Noah Paalman  
7. Alba Ramirez  
8. Madison Long  
9. Camryn Broz  

### History of the Series

The two programs have met twice:

- April 29, 2021 (NAIA Tournament): Keiser won 6-0.  
- Earlier this season at Keiser: Cumberlands won 1-0.

### How They Stack Up

**Offense**

- Keiser: 4th in NAIA, 78 goals (10th in scoring)  
- Cumberlands: 21st in NAIA, 64 goals (25th in scoring)

**Defense**

- Both ranked 34th in NAIA  
- Cumberlands: 14 shutouts (4th in NAIA)  
- Keiser: 12 shutouts (9th in NAIA)

### Key Players

- **Keiser**  
  - Lauren Riley – 19 goals  
  - Maria Vilhena – 13 goals  
  - Blanca Bachi – 10 goals  
  - Mio Tanaka (GK) – 19 matches, 13 goals allowed  

- **Cumberlands**  
  - Nicole Araujo – 11 goals  
  - Morgan White (GK) – 24 matches, 9 shutouts, 18 goals allowed
