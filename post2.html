The Men's and Women's soccer teams at the University of the Cumberlands are gearing up for an exciting season kicking off on Thursday August 21st.

On Wednesday the teams received their preseason rankings. The Men's team was ranked #4 in the country while the Women's team was ranked #8 in the country.

The Women's team will kick off their season on August 21st against Missouri Baptist University in St. Louis at 2 PM EST. The Men's team will face Grace College at 7 PM in Winona Lake, Indiana.
