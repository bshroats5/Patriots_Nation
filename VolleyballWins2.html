Pats Volleyball Team Wins 2

Published on August 22, 2025 by Admin

The Cumberlands (UC) volleyball team began their season with a perfect 2-0 start at the Onset Tournament. Match 1: Victory over Indiana Tech (3-2)

The Patriots won a hard-fought, five-set match against Indiana Tech. After dropping the first set, they rallied to win 3 of the next 4. The five scores were 14-25, 25-23, 25-20, 23-25, 15-3. Key performances included:

  • Sara Zampedri: 17 kills, 10 digs, and 6 aces.
  • Davey Slaughter: A career-high 35 digs.
  • Savanah Jolly: 38 assists and 18 digs.

Match 2: Victory over West Virginia Tech (3-0)

UC dominated their second match, sweeping the Bears in three straight sets with scores of 25-12, 25-10, and 25-12. Standout players were:

  • Brynnley Bryant: Led the offense with 17 kills.
  • Alana Shiery: Surpassed her career-high from the first match with 10 kills and 4 blocks.
  • Savanah Jolly: Set a new career-high with 45 assists.

What's Next? The team will continue playing in the Onset tournament on Saturday, facing the University of Saint Francis and Bethel.
